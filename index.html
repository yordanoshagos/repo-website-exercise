My First Week at AkiraChix

Introduction

My first week at AkiraChix is the first boarding experience I have ever had, it has been an incredible journey filled with new experiences, learning opportunities, and exciting challenges. Students gathered from different places with different cultural and educational experiences. And I am excited to be part of this big community. AkiraChix is a place for a qualified experince by empowering young womens. Here are some highlights:

Highlights of the Week

  1. Meeting amazing mentors and fellow students
  2. Exploring innovative tech projects
  3. Participating in team-building activities
  4. Learning about the inspiring mission of AkiraChix
  5. Experinced new different types of foods

Good Experiences

  • The staff members are very friendly and welcoming.
  • It brings people together from various nations and cultures.
  • Learning new languages and cultures, exchanging experiences.
  • Interesting classes and a new way of learning.
  • Different way of serving the meal

Not So Good Experiences

  • The classroom closes at 11 p.m, and we can't come anytime we want.
  • Not having full WiFi access inside dorms
  • We do not take Sundays off from our other responsibilities.
  • To get used to the taste of ugali

Conclusion

My first week at AkiraChix has been incredibly life-changing. Every day has been an educational and personal development journey, thanks to the friendly and helpful staff and the diverse range of cultures. Even though there are some difficulties, like the short class hours and WiFi availability, the benefits greatly exceed any drawbacks. I sincerely look forward to the journey ahead, continuing my education, and developing enduring relationships with my mentors and peers. I am honored to be a part of AkiraChix, a thriving community that is more than just a place to learn.
